Lamb Doner Kebab

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pita bread
  • Salad such lettuce, tomatoes, onions, etc.
  • Tzatziki sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix meat, onions, garlic, spices and salt and pepper together
  2. Shape the mixture into a long, thick log shape
  3. preheat grill to medium-high heat
  4. Cook lamb kebab for 10 - 15 minutes turning occasionally until cooked through and slightly charred
  5. warm pita bread in the oven or grill
  6. take the kebab meat out of the grill to rest
  7. slice the lamb into thin strips
  8. fill the warm pita with salad and meat
  9. Enjoy!
